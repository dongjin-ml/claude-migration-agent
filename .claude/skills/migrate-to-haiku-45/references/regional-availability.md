- **Available** but as **regional enablement**
- Requires enablement before customer can access
- Public documentation only lists global endpoint, which causes confusion for FSI customers
- Coordinate with your Anthropic account team for enablement

## Verification Process

1. Check public documentation for listed regions
2. Contact your Anthropic account team for regional enablement availability (not listed publicly)
3. Confirm enablement status with the customer's account team
4. Validate endpoint connectivity before committing to a migration timeline

## FSI Customer Considerations
- FSI (Financial Services Industry) customers often require regional deployment for regulatory compliance
- Always verify regional availability before committing to a migration timeline
- Don't rely solely on public docs — check with Anthropic account team for regional enablement availability
- Common FSI regions with potential constraints: asia-southeast1 (Singapore), asia-northeast1 (Tokyo), europe-west1 (Belgium)
